Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account on LuminaReads?

You can sign up for a free account by visiting the main page and clicking the "Join Now" button. Simply provide your name, email address, and create a password. Once verified, you'll have full access to the platform and can immediately start writing reviews and connecting with other readers.

Can I edit or delete my reviews after posting?

Yes! You can edit or delete your reviews at any time from your profile page. Simply navigate to "My Reviews," find the review you'd like to modify, and click the edit or delete button. Changes are saved immediately, and other members will see the updated version.

How are books organized on the browse page?

Books are organized by genre, rating, and publication date. Use our filtering sidebar to narrow down your search by selecting specific genres (Fiction, Sci-Fi, Biography, etc.) or minimum rating (3+ stars, 4+ stars). You can also sort results by popularity, top-rated, or newest first.

What should I include in my book review?

A great review includes: (1) your overall rating, (2) a brief summary without major spoilers, (3) what you liked about the book, (4) any criticisms or areas for improvement, and (5) personal reflection on how the book impacted you. We encourage thoughtful analysis over simple summaries. Use spoiler tags when discussing plot points.

Can I follow other reviewers?

Absolutely! When you visit another member's profile, you can click the "Follow" button to keep up with their latest reviews and activity. You'll see their posts in your personalized feed. You can unfollow anyone at any time. Following is a great way to discover new perspectives on books you love.

What is the community moderation policy?

We maintain a respectful community by moderating content that violates our guidelines: no hate speech, harassment, or spam. All reviews should focus on the book's content, not the author's personal beliefs or appearance. Members who repeatedly violate guidelines may have their accounts suspended or permanently removed.

Is LuminaReads available on mobile?

Yes! LuminaReads is fully responsive and works beautifully on smartphones, tablets, and desktop computers. You can browse books, write reviews, and manage your profile on any device. No app installation required— just visit our website in your mobile browser.
